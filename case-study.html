The Challenge

Village Hidden in Iron had a strong product line but lacked a structured paid media system to scale customer acquisition. They needed consistent volume, low CPC, and a retargeting engine that converted warm traffic into buyers.

We deployed a three-phase funnel combining Advantage+ Shopping for cold acquisition, a conversion retargeting campaign for warm audiences, and ongoing creative testing. CPC was driven to $0.80 — exceptionally efficient for the fitness vertical.
